PROJECTS, WORKSHOPS, PAPER-PRESENTATIONS

PROJECT: DIGITIZATION OF LAB REPORTS USING OCR

This application digitizes the lab reports from health care center using OCR , stores the data in the cloud as per Insurance companies standard formats. It also generates the trend charts of various reports.

PROJECT: TRAFFIC LIGHT AUTOMATION USING SOUND SENSOR

This hardware interface installed at Traffic light posts receives Siren frequency of emergency vehicles and switches the traffic signal to green providing signal clearance for emergency vehicles like Ambulance, Fire Engine and VVIPs cars.

PROJECT: SMART Ed ( SMART EDUCATION )

Developed a web portal which connects students and teacher / mentor from different locations for free education / clarification on any subjects. The portal has a list of volunteer teachers / mentors mapped to students from different places.

PAPER-PRESENTATION: INDIA Vs POLLUTION :

Developed an App which bridges the common man and Municipal authorities by providing information, location & picture about pollution, waste and other environmental hazards pertaining to the locality. It also tracks the action taken by the officials and provides the timely service to Municipal authorities
